How much do you know about popular rock bands?

Guns N Roses

Easily one of the most recognisable hard rock bands on the plant, Guns N Roses never fails to deliver a good song. Formed in Los Angeles, California in 1985, Guns N Roses has become an undeniably incredible band over the years.

Guns N Roses started out with:

Vocalist, Axl Rose

Lead Guitarist, Slash

Rythm Guitarist, Izzy Stradlin

Bassist, Duff McKagan

Drummer, Steven Adler

Welcome to the Jungle

Welcome to the Jungle is a song about Los Angeles. In particular it brings to light the dark side of the city that many people encounter when moving there to pursue fame. Axl Rose wrote the lyrics while he was in Seattle, this gave him some perspective on the size of Los Angeles.

Live and Let Die

Guns N Rose's Live and Let Die is actually a cover done by the band Wings with Paul McCartney (the Beatles). The original song was released in 1973 as the theme song to the James Bond film 'Live and Let Die'. Axl Rose actually referred to Live and Let Die as Welcome to the Jungle 2.

Paradise City

Like Welcome to the Jungle, Paradise City is inspired by Los Angeles, the city where the band was living at the time. Except the chorus reflects more on Axl Rose's memories of the Midwest. This is the first song where every single member of Guns N Rose's original lineup is listed as writers.

The part of the song where Axl sings, "Where the grass is green, and the girls are pretty" is about when Axl Rose and his family would travel to Bloomington, Indiana.
